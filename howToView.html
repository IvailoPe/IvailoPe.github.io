Card Combinations

1 - Blinds&HoleCards

Dealer is choosen, 2 players next to the dealer post blinds to start the pot. Each player is dealt two personal cards. Players can call,raise or fold. Betting stops when all bets are the same

2 - The Flop

Three community cards are dealt face up. Players options are to call,raise or fold. Betting stops when all bets are the same

3 - The Turn

A fourth community card is dealt and a new round of betting follows. Betting stops when all bets are the same

4 & 5 - TheRiver&ShowDown

A fifth community card is dealt and players bet for the last time. Remaining players reveal their hands, players create combination with their cards and the community cards. The strongest hand wins
